The Inner Self

THE EGO:

An image we create in our heads to protect our self-esteem.

INNER CHILD:

Unhealed wounds and suppressed emotions.

SUBCONSCIOUS:

Conditionings that were programmed during childhood.

CONSCIOUS:

Aware and authentic self beyond conditioning.

The ego is the part of us that is:

  • sensitive to criticism and being misunderstood
  • constantly trying to prove itself
  • fear based (anxiety due to past experiences)

The inner child is the part of us that:

  • still lives within in us; is desperate to be seen and heard
  • has unmet needs (love and care)
  • looks for approval from family, friends, etc.

The subconscious mind is the part of us that:

  • has trust issues
  • doubts one's self
  • has a competitive mindset ("I'm better than you.")
  • uses about 95% of the brain

The conscious mind is the part of us that is:

  • aware of our actions and is able to control how we react to things
  • supportive of others
  • able to see self-worth
  • love based
  • 5% of the brain
